Korean Fried Rice

Description

Korean Fried rice is a quick fufullinig meal that can be created in minutes wtih very little ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Left over Rice
  • Your choice of instant korean noodles Most preffered is Shin Ramyun
  • choice of meat
  • and your choice of vegetables

Steps

  1. Put the left over rice into the pan
  2. While the left over rice is cooking in the pan start cooking the ramyun as you would with cup noodles
  3. once that finishes remove some of the liquid from the ramyun and pour the noodles into the pan with the cooking rice
  4. stir stir and stir once the noodles and rice is combined together you can add your vegetables and meat now
  5. while you do that also add some ofhte liquid into the pan to get the taste of the ramyun
  6. once its done grab the ramyun cup and pour the korean fried rice into the ramyunu cup and enjoy!
